Experience

Professional Background

Currently, I am working at a restaurant on Mercer Island. This role has provided me with valuable experience in customer service, teamwork, and time management. My responsiblities include taking orders, handling customer inquiries, and ensuring a pleasant dining experience for all patrons. Working in a fast-paced environment had hones my ability to stay calm under pressure and deliver excellent service consistently.

In addition to my restaurant job, I have a substantial background in nannying. A few years ago, I nannied special needs boy, which taught me patience, empathy, and specialised caregiving skills. Last summer, I nannied twins, managing their daily routines and engaging them in educational and recreational activities. These experiences have greatly enhanced my multitasking abilities and my capacity to create a safe, nurturing environment for children.

Since my freshman year of high school, I have been volunteering with The Friendship Circle of WA. This is a nonprofit organisation focuses on providing support and companionship to children and young adults with special needs. My role involves organising activities, mentoring participants, and fostering a sense of community. Volunteering here has been incredibly rewarding, reinforcing my commitment to helping others and developing strong interpersonal skills.

My diverse work and volunteer experiences have equipped me with a broad skill set that includes customer service, caregiving, and community support. These roles have not only developed my professional abilities but also shaped my personal growth. I am confident that the skills and insights I have gained will be invaluable as I pursue my career in business and entrepreneurship. I am eager to leverage these experiences to make a meaningful impact in my future pursuits.
