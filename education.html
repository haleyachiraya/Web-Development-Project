Education

Bachelor of Business Administration

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration at Gonzaga University, with a concentration in Entrepreneurship and a minor in Psychology. As a junior, I am on track to graduate in May of 2026. My coursework has equipped me with a strong foundation in business principles, strategic thinking, and an understanding of human behaviour. Key courses include Business Management Information Systems, Entrepreneurship, and various psychology classes.

I had the incredible opportunity to participate in a study abroad program in Madrid, Spain, for five months. This experience significantly broadened my horizons, allowing me to gain knowledge about different cultures and develop a greater understanding of myself. It was a transformative period during which I matured and became more open-minded, skills that are invaluable both personally and professionally.

I graduated from Sammamish High School in 2022. My high school education provided a strong academic foundation and numerous opportunities for personal growth and leadership development.

My educational journey has been characterised by a commitment to learning, personaly growth, and embracing new experiences. These experiences have not only prepared me academically but have also shaped me into a well-rounded individual ready to take on the challenges of the business world. I am excited to apply my knowledge, skills, and unique perspective to future endeavors in entrepreneurship and beyond.
